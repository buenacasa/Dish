The Dish
The Dish is a beloved segment from Jack Riccardi’s KTSA radio talk show, The Jack Riccardi Show. Featuring raw, unfiltered opinions from real callers (praise or zing), it captures the authentic, conversational charm of talk radio, setting it apart from typical restaurant review sites. Imagine sharing a new find with a friend at work or home — that’s the spirit of The Dish. Whether you’re a San Antonio local or just passing through South Texas, discover household favorites, hidden gems, and more — from quick food truck bites to special-occasion fine dining — all through genuine, caller-driven recommendations.
