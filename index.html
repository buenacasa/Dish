The Dish

The Dish is a beloved segment from Jack Riccardi’s KTSA radio talk show, featuring raw, unfiltered opinions from real callers on San Antonio and South Texas restaurants. Picture sharing an exciting new discovery or old favorite with family and friends – that’s the heart of The Dish. Whether you’re a local or just passing through South Texas, explore go-to spots, hidden gems, and more – from quick food truck bites to special-occasion fine dining – all through genuine, caller-driven recommendations.
